Home Page

The mental illness that we are going to focus on is depression.Depression is a condition that makes someone feel sad and hopeless most of the time, and they lose interest in things they used to enjoy. This can make them avoid socializing with friends and family, which can worsen their mood. Depression can also lead to drug abuse, which is the use of substances to get high and temporarily relieve stress. However, drug abuse can have harmful effects on the body and mind, and it can create more problems in the long run. Depression can also increase the risk of suicide, which is when someone ends their life prematurely.
