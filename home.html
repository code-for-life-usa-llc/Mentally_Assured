The main 3 mental illnesses we are going to be talking about is depression, suicide,
and drug abuse. Mental illnesses are different for everyone. For example: some people experience their
depression by being sad and alone in their rooms all day, while others pretend to be happy and joyful
while hurting. Depression is a condition someone exhibits by being sad all the time and having no energy, so they avoid
going out with friends. Drug abuse is usally caused by depression and anger issues, which is another type of mental illness,
Drug abuse is using drugs to feel high and alleviate stress for only a little bit of time, which often leads to awful consequences. Depression often leads to suicide,
which is when someone ends their life too early.
