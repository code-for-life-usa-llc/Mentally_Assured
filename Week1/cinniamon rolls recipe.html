cinniamon rolls recipe

  1. first you need milk either 2%, whole milk or almond milk

  2. next you will need granulated sugar this will make sure to sweeten the cinniamon rolls

  3. after you do that you will need quick rise yeast that will help the cinniamon rolls rise faster if you don't have quick rise yeast you can active dry yeast but the cinniamon rolls will slightly take longer to rise

  4. next you will need butter, you can either use salted butter or unsalted butter. you will need it for the dough, filling and icing

  5. next you will need 1 eggs + plus 1 egg yulk for the perfect texture and rich consistency in the dough

  6. next you will need bread flour to help keep the cinnamon rolls soft and fluffy

  7. after adding the bread flour you will need to add brown sugar but if you want you can add dark brown sugar for more robust molasses sweet flavor in those cinnamon swirls

    8. next you will need salt because without salt tastes very plain and in addition, salt also helps to control yeast in cinnamon roll recipes
    lastly for the icing, you will just need cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar and vanilla.

cinniamon roll ingredients

milk
granulated sugar
quick rise yeast or active dry yeast
butter
1 egg and 1 egg yulk
bread flour
brown sugar or dark brown sugar
salt
lastly for the icing you will just need cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar and vanilla
