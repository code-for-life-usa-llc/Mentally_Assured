Drug Abuse

What are some effects of Drug Abuse?

The use of drugs can lead to lung cancer or a psychological addiction. These are only some of the things that drug abuse can lead to, but there are even more consequences, like anxiety or even death.



What Causes Drug Use?

Drugs have been a big problem in the world for many years and often, drug abuse is caused by depression, peer pressure, and stress. These are the most common causes of drug abuse.




What to do if You are Thinking of Drug Use?

If you are thinking of using drugs or if you are already using drugs then you can call 866-545-4102 for help.
